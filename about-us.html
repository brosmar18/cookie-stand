About Us

Salmon Cookies Co is a family-owned business that was founded in 2015 with the goal of providing unique and delicious cookies made from fresh salmon. We pride ourselves on using only the highest quality ingredients and delivering exceptional customer service.

Our Philosophy

At Salmon Cookies Co, we believe that food should be both delicious and nutritious. That's why we've created a line of savory salmon cookies that are packed with protein and wholesome ingredients. We're passionate about using fresh, locally-sourced salmon and organic, non-GMO flour to create our delicious treats.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate foodies who share a love of salmon and baking. We're committed to delivering the best possible cookies to our customers and are always experimenting with new flavors and recipes. We take pride in being a small, family-owned business and are grateful for the opportunity to share our passion with the world.
